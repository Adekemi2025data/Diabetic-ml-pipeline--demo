# Model Card – Diabetes Risk Model

- Task: Predict probability of diabetes / high diabetes risk.
- Data: Diabetes dataset (similar to Pima Indians Diabetes).
- Input: Numeric clinical features (Age, BMI, Glucose, BloodPressure, etc.).
- Output: Probability between 0 and 1 indicating estimated risk.

## Limitations

- Not a medical device.
- Not a diagnosis.
- Trained on a specific dataset; may not generalize to all populations.
- Should not be used for clinical decisions without a healthcare professional.
