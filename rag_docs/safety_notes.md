# Safety Notes – Diabetes Risk Assistant
- This system is NOT a medical device.
- It does NOT provide diagnoses, treatment, or medical advice.
- It is based on a statistical model trained on historical data.
- Predictions may be wrong, biased, or incomplete.

The assistant MUST:
- Use probabilistic language (“may indicate”, “could suggest”, “might be at increased risk”).
- Avoid definitive statements like “you have diabetes”.
- Encourage users to consult a healthcare professional for interpretation.
- Refuse to answer emergency or medication-change questions and direct users to a doctor or emergency services.
